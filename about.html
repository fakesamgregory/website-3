WHO WE ARE

J&TG's team is made up of multidisciplinary experts who bring years of unrivalled technical and creative expertise to each and every project. From the very start of any engagement, the focus is always on creating exceptional design, solid code and the most immersive experiences that's firmly aligned to core strategic objectives.

Samuel Gregory

Founder & Creative Technologist

Using his years of experience in the creative industry and building websites for the likes of Netflix, Corning, and Conde-Nast, Samuel leads teams with his out-of-the-box thinking and technical know-how.

Samuel founded J&TG in 2019 as a remote-first consultancy and built the company into offering world-class experiential websites for clients around the world.

Neil Noakes

Product Strategy Consultant

Neil has an unparalleled eye for detail, precision and strategic thinking working with behemoth clients such as NHS, Ministry of Justice, amongst others to Jupiter and the Giraffe as part of our strategic Lean App Strategy onboarding phase.

Chris Constantine

Experience Producer

Chris has been crafting experiences for major brands for over 20 years, working across sectors including automotive, health, finance, FMCG and luxury travel.

Bringing businesses a closer understanding of their customer needs, Chris is passionate about user-centred design, founded on insightful research and validated through rigorous testing.

Lawrence Lam

Experience Designer

Laurence (Larry) Lam brings exceptional craft and vision to lead the experience design team here at J&TG. Having worked with enterprise clients such as QED Technology, BBC, Ministry of Sound, B&Q, and most recently UBS who set the most challenging environments that test the fusion of creativity and functionality.

Paul Gosnell

Technical Producer

Paul's deep technical knowledge and experience at a director level allows him to oversee and produce the development team and encourages robust, maintainable and scalable digital products. Being a serial entrepreneur himself, managing his own startup Fitlink as drawing from his work with IBM pays dividends in quality and efficiency.
